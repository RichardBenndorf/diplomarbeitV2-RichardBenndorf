Collateral clauses and instruction on right to appeal

Collateral clause

All equipment which corresponds to the approved type is to be identified according to the applied regulation.

The individual production of serial fabrication must be in exact accordance with the approval documents. Changes in the individual production are only allowed with express consent of the Kraftfahrt-Bundesamt.

Changes in the name of the company, the address and the manufacturing plant as well as one of the parties given the authority to delivery or authorised representative named when the approval was granted is immediately disclosed to the Kraftfaht-Bundesanat.

Breach of this regulation can lead to recall of the approval and moreover can be legally prosecuted.

The renewal expires if it is returned or withdrawn or if the type approved no longer complies with the legal requirements. The revocation can be made if the demanded requirements for issuance and the continuance of the approval no longer exist, if the holder of the appoval violates the duties involved in the approval, also to the extent that they result from the assigned conditions to this approval, or if it determined that the approved types does not comply with the requirements of traffic safety or environmental protection.

The Kraftfahr-Bundesaat may check the proper exercise of the conferred authority taken from this approval at any time. In particular this means the compliant production as well the measures for conformity of production. For this purpose samples can be taken or have taken. The employees or the representatives of the Kraftraht-Bunestaat may get unhindered access to the production and storage facilities.

The conferred authority contained with issuance of this approval is not transferable. Trade mark rights of third parties are not affected with this approval.
